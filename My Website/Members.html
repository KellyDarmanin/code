Founding Members

Alexia Scholl

Alexia is a 24 year old graphic designer who had a bachelor in Graphic design and interactive media. Her passion for design and marketing led her to collaborate with some of her colleagues to open their own buisines called Tesoro.

Sabrina Wood

Sabrina Wood is a twenty two year old artist who loves sharing her creations online. Her creative side started at a very young age, she states that her mother would give her a canvas, paint, glitters and beads and she would spend hours creating "interesting" creations. She is the mind behind most of our designs.
