Materials

Choosing the material for each of our products was a challange, because we have so many different products and we had to choose the best material for every different product. The best material depended on the best quality with the lowest price, that is how we were able to price our products at a reasonable price.

When choosing the materials, intense research was done. We experimented with different materials until we found the ones best suited for our buisiness. We now have the honur of serving the customers with the best materials for their products.
